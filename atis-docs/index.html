üìñ What is ATIS?

Advanced Traveler Information Systems (ATIS) are intelligent transportation systems designed to provide travelers with real-time, accurate, and comprehensive information to help them make informed decisions about their travel. ATIS integrates multiple data sources including transit schedules, traffic conditions, weather forecasts, and incident reports to deliver personalized trip planning and navigation assistance.

Our ATIS implementation is a comprehensive web-based platform that combines real-time transit data, multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA), and intelligent routing algorithms to optimize travel experiences across multiple transportation modes including buses, trains, walking, and cycling.

üéØ Goals in Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)

1. Enhance Traveler Experience

Provide users with comprehensive, real-time information to make informed travel decisions, reducing uncertainty and stress during trips.

2. Optimize Transportation Efficiency

Utilize multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA) to recommend optimal routes based on time, cost, comfort, and reliability factors.

3. Reduce Traffic Congestion

Distribute traffic across alternative routes and encourage use of public transportation through better information and planning tools.

4. Improve Safety

Provide real-time alerts about traffic incidents, weather conditions, and safety information to help travelers avoid hazardous situations.

5. Promote Sustainable Transportation

Encourage use of public transit and active transportation modes (walking, cycling) by making them more accessible and convenient.

6. Enable Data-Driven Decision Making

Collect and analyze travel patterns to help transportation authorities make informed infrastructure and service improvements.

7. Increase System Reliability

Provide accurate, up-to-date information about delays, disruptions, and alternative options to maintain traveler confidence.

üìã System Architecture

Three-Tier Architecture

Presentation Layer: React-based web application providing intuitive user interface

Application Layer: FastAPI backend handling business logic, authentication, and data processing

Data Layer: PostgreSQL database for user data, preferences, and cached transit information

Key Components

  • Authentication Service: JWT-based user authentication with optional MFA support
  • Trip Planning Engine: Multi-modal route calculation with MCDA optimization
  • Real-Time Data Service: Integration with transit APIs for live departure information
  • Incident Detection System: Traffic and service disruption monitoring
  • Weather Service: Location-based weather forecast integration
  • Export Service: PDF generation for itinerary export

Real-time transit information and multi-modal trip planning to enhance public transit accessibility.

Decision Support Systems

MCDA algorithms provide intelligent decision support for route selection and trip planning.
